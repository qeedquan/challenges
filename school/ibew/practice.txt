*** Questions

Q: What is the next number in the following series of numbers?
7 10 15 22 31
A: 42

Q: Consider the following formula:
A = (B - 2) * (C + 5)
If A = 6 and B = 0, what is the value of C?
A: -8

Q: Factor the Polynomial
x^2 - 3*x - 18
A: (x+3)*(x-6) (Sum is equal to coefficient B, product is equal to coefficient C)

Q: Evaluate expression:
x = 11
x^2 - 15x + 56
A: 12

Q: Evaluate the value of the unknown variable:
Y = 27
Z = Y / 9
X = Y * Z
A: 81
