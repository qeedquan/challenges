*** Terms

Probability Mass Function (PMF):    Discrete Probability Distribution Function
Probability Density Function (PDF): Continuous Probability Distribution Function

Moments - Quantitative measures related to the shape of the function, kind of like a derivative in some sense
1st - Expected Value/Mean
2nd - Variance
3rd - Skewness
4th - Kurtosis
5th - Hyperskewness
6th - Hypertailedness
